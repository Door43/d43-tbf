====== Salt Sea ======

===== Facts: =====

The Salt sea is also called the Dead Sea.

  * The Salt Sea or Salton Sea is located in the middle of Israel's territories.
  * The Salt Sea is fed from the North by the Jordan river.
  * It was a place where King Herod had a health resort.
