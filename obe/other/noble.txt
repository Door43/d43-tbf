====== noble ======

===== Definition: =====

A person that belongs to an elite political or social class.

  * A nobleman was an officer of the state, a close servant to the King.
  * In the New Testamant Jesus healed a nobleman's son because he had faith. The nobleman named Chuza was said to be the steward of Herod, meaning he had access to the King's property and wealth.

(See also: other)
