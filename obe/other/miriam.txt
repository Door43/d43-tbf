====== Miriam ======

===== Facts: =====

Miriam was the biological sister of Moses and Aaron.

  * She was charged with watching the basket bearing Moses, then brought his mother to serve as his nurse.
  * She was very opposed to the marriage of Moses to a Cushite woman and may have led a rebellion against him.
  * Later, Miriam was struck with leprosy when she displeased God, but was healed after Moses interceeded for her.
