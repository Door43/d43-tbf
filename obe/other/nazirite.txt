====== Nazirite, Nazirite vow ======

===== Facts: =====

A Nazirite in Old Testament times was a man who had taken the Nazirite vow.

  * The vow, or promise would be takena man as an act of devotion to God.
  * The vow involved refraining from any drink or food made from grapes and not cutting his hair for a certain length of time.
  * When the time period of refrain was complete, that is, the fullfillment of the vow, the Nazirite would present himself before the priest and provide an offering, including the cutting and burning of his hair.
