====== assign, assigned ======

===== Facts: =====

To assign is to appoint or designate.

  * The prophet Samuel foretold that King Saul would assign the best young men of Israel to be military subjects.
  * Moses assigned the promised land by divisions for Israel to inhabit.
  * Under the Old Testament law, certain tribes of Israel were assigned to serve as priests, artists, singers and builders.
