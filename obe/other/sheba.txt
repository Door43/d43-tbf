====== Sheba ======

===== Facts: =====

An ancient civilization in southern Arabia.

  * Sheba existed near present day Yemen from 1200 B.C. to 275 A.D.
  * The Queen of Sheba came and visited King Solomon when she heard of his fame and wisdom.
