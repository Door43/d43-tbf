====== lover ======

===== Definition: =====

A lover is the giver and the recipient of the basic ingredient of all human relationships, love.

  * Those who are lovers are characterized by devotion, responsibility and loyalty to the one loved.
  * In the Song of Solomon this term has a sexual connotation; in the proper Biblical sense the participants are a husband and wife.

(See also: other)
