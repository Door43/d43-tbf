====== magnify ======

===== Facts: =====

To magnify is to exalt or draw attention to someone or something.

  * When used in the Bible the term usually refers to exalting a King or God Himself.
  * In the gospel of Luke Mary sings a song in which she says "my soul will magnify the Lord." This meant that she was glorifying Him and giving Him praise.
  * The future antichrist king described by the Prophet Daniel will magnify himself above all known dieties and demand to be worshipped.
