====== alarm ======

===== Facts: =====

To be alarmed is to be frightened, often in response to circumstances.

  * In the Old Testament Jehoshepat King of Judah was alarmed when he heard that the Moabites were planning to attack Judah.
  * In the New Testament Jesus said to not be alarmed when we hear about wars and rumors of wars in the last days.
