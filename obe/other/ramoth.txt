====== Ramoth ======

===== Facts: =====

A famous city in the mountains of Gilead; often called RamothGilead.

  * The city belonged to Gad and was assigned as a city of refuge.
  * At RamothAhab died, Joram was wounded, and Jehu was anointed king of Israel.
